Quick help

Purchases and restore

If premium is not showing up, make sure you are signed into the same Apple Account used for the purchase, then use the in-app restore option if it is available.

Apple handles billing and refunds for App Store purchases, so purchase history is the best first place to check.

iCloud sync

iCloud sync depends on your Apple Account, iCloud availability, and any premium sync settings you have enabled. If sync seems delayed, confirm iCloud Drive is available and reopen the app.

Ads and privacy

TierListr may use Google AdMob and consent messaging in supported regions. For privacy details, see the Privacy Policy.

Bugs and feedback

If something feels wrong, send a short description, the steps you took, and any screenshots that help explain it. Feature suggestions are welcome too.
